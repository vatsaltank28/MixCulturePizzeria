Our Story

Where Cultures
Meet on a Pizza.

Born from a deep love for crafting experiences, Mix Culture began long before 2021. From stepping into the world of events at 16 to chasing creative dreams across cities, the idea slowly took shape—driven by passion, risk, and vision.

In 2021, with just three people and a bold belief, Mix Culture came to life. What started as a dream is now a space where flavors, cultures, and stories come together.

At our core is the authentic Chicago-style deep-dish pizza—but our soul goes beyond borders. We blend the richness of Italian tradition with the vibrant, street-style energy of New York, creating something truly our own.

This isn’t just pizza. It’s a culture, built with heart.

— Founder, Mix Culture
